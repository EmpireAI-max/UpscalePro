Help Center

Welcome to the UpscalePro Help Center. Here you'll find resources to help you get the most out of our image upscaling service.

Getting Started

To begin upscaling your images, simply follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to the Upload section on our homepage
  2. Drag and drop your image or click to browse your files
  3. Select your desired resolution (2K or 4K)
  4. Wait for the processing to complete
  5. Preview and download your upscaled image

Supported Formats

UpscalePro supports the following image formats:

  • JPEG/JPG
  • PNG
  • WebP
  • BMP
  • TIFF

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the upscaling process work?

UpscalePro uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze your image and intelligently increase its resolution while preserving details and reducing artifacts. The process enhances image quality by predicting and adding missing details that would be present in a higher-resolution version of the image.

What's the maximum file size I can upload?

The maximum file size for uploads is 20MB. This limit ensures optimal processing times and quality results. If your image is larger than 20MB, we recommend resizing it before uploading.

How long does the upscaling process take?

Processing time depends on the size and complexity of your image, but typically takes between 15-60 seconds. Larger images or those with more detail may take slightly longer to process.

Will upscaling reduce image quality?

No, our AI-powered upscaling is specifically designed to enhance image quality. Unlike traditional resizing methods that can cause blurriness or pixelation, our technology intelligently adds detail to create a sharper, clearer image at higher resolutions.

Can I use upscaled images commercially?

Yes, you retain all rights to your original images and the upscaled versions. You can use upscaled images for personal or commercial purposes, provided you have the appropriate rights to the original image.

What happens to my images after processing?

We take your privacy seriously. Your images are processed on our servers but are automatically deleted within 24 hours of processing. We do not store, share, or use your images for any purpose other than providing the upscaling service you requested.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely through encrypted channels.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel during a billing period, you will retain access to premium features until the end of that billing cycle.
